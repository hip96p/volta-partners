About

Deep Expertise,
Operational Agility

Volta Partners, a division of Fluent Africa Limited, provides firm-level advisory services for development finance institutions and institutional investors. Founded by David Gabriel — a veteran investment banker and DFI strategist who has served at IFC, the African Development Bank, and led transactions across 18 countries — the firm brings together a curated network of senior experts for complex mandates.

Our approach combines deep institutional knowledge with operational agility. We assemble bespoke expert teams for each engagement, drawing from a network of seasoned professionals with DFI, private equity, and development finance experience across Africa and emerging markets.

Service Areas

What We Do

Specialized advisory spanning the full lifecycle of development finance — from transaction structuring to institutional capacity building.

Transaction Advisory & Structuring

Project preparation, financial modeling, deal structuring for infrastructure, energy, and PPP transactions. Support for project finance, blended finance vehicles, and catalytic capital mobilization.

Investment Program Design

Design and operationalization of investment programs, special funds, and financial intermediary vehicles for DFIs and sovereign investors.

Digital Economy & Financial Inclusion

Digital transformation strategies, fintech ecosystem development, digital payments infrastructure, and financial inclusion program design.

ESG, Climate & Sustainable Finance

Climate finance strategies, ESG integration frameworks, Principles for Responsible Banking implementation, green bond structuring.

Private Sector Development

PPP advisory, investment climate reform, business environment strengthening, and investment promotion strategies.

Institutional Capacity Building

Training, knowledge management, and organizational effectiveness programs for DFIs and public sector institutions.
